Works Without Internet

DukaMate is designed to work offline, so you can manage your shop even in areas with limited connectivity.

  • Local Data Storage

    All your data is stored securely on your device, so you can access it anytime.

  • No Internet Required

    Process sales, update inventory, and manage customers without an internet connection.

  • Sync When Connected

    When internet is available, DukaMate automatically backs up your data to the cloud.
